# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Private company employees across departments: inventory staff managing stock and supplies, payroll staff handling payslip distribution, department heads submitting and approving requisitions, warehouse teams tracking fabric and tailoring requests, IT administrators managing users and system configuration, and super admins overseeing operations and analytics. Each role has a tailored workspace focused on their primary tasks.

## Product Purpose

A unified internal operations platform that streamlines inventory management, stock tracking, employee requisitions, payroll processing, and fabric/tailoring workflows. Success means reducing the time spent on manual tracking and paperwork, eliminating stock discrepancies, and giving every role a clear, efficient path through their daily tasks.

## Brand Personality

Professional, trustworthy, efficient. The interface should feel like a reliable tool — precise without being cold, complete without being overwhelming. Every interaction communicates competence and clarity.

## Anti-references

Avoid overly complex enterprise UIs. No cluttered dashboards, dense data dumps without hierarchy, or SAP/Oracle-style interfaces where finding anything takes three clicks. No generic SaaS templates — the design should feel purpose-built for this tool, not drop-shipped from a template library.

## Design Principles

- **Clarity first**: Every screen answers "what do I do here?" within seconds. Hierarchy, labeling, and whitespace lead the eye to the primary action.
- **Efficiency as a feature**: Reduce clicks, surface what's relevant per role, and make repetitive tasks fast. The tool gets out of the way.
- **Trust through consistency**: Predictable patterns, reliable feedback, and stable UI — users should never wonder if something worked.
- **Precision without density**: Show enough information to act, not everything possible. Use progressive disclosure for depth.
- **Professional restraint**: Clean, purposeful design. No decoration for its own sake, no gratuitous animations. Motion and color serve function.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA compliance. Adequate color contrast, keyboard-navigable interfaces, screen reader support, and reduced motion support.
